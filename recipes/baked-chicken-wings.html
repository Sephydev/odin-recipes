Baked Chicken Wings

Description

These baked chicken wings are easy, crispy, and delicious! I normally serve them with a side of rice, chicken gravy, and a vegetable or salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine olive oil, garlic, chili powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag.
  3. Seal and shake to combine.
  4. Add chicken wings.
  5. Reseal and shake to coat.
  6. Arrange chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  7. Cook ckicken wings in the preheated oven until crips and cooked through, about 30 to 45 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
