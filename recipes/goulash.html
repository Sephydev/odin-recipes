Quick and Easy Goulash

Description

This goulash with egg nouudles is a homemade meal easy enough for a busy college student! Hearty beef and cheese with noodles and corn.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (26 pound) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add egg noodles and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain.
  4. Brown beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Drain extra grease.
  6. Mix in spaghetti sauce, corn, onion, and cheese with the beef over medium heat.
  7. Add in noodles and stir together to heat through.
